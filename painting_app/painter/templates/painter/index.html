AI Virtual Painter

Paint using gestures and control with your hands!

Gesture Instructions

    Index Finger Up (Draw Mode): Enable drawing on the canvas. Lines are drawn with adjustable thickness.

    Two Fingers Up (Selection Mode): Activate color selection using the index finger's x-coordinate.

    Thumb and Index Pinch (Thickness Control): Adjust the drawing thickness for precise control.

    Five Fingers Up (Pen Up): Move the brush without drawing.

    All Fingers Down (Clear Canvas): Clear the canvas by resetting the overlay.

    Thumb, Index and Middle Fingers (Download): Download the canvas - i.e. the drawings and the webcam shot it is drawn over - after a brief countdown.
